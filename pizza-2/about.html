Our Story

Pizza Pizzaria was started during the 16th Century in modern day Utah. Our first pizzas were sold out of a walk-in closet in Midvale. Once word got out about our pizzas we outgrew that closet and had to expand. First we moved to a chain of intertubes in the Jordan River, and then to an abandoned school bus, but both became problomatic with our fireclay ovens. We eventually settled in our current location in Herriman.

Our pizza is made by an elite team of pizzateers from across the country. Our founder was born and raised in Utah and became obsessed with pizzas from a young age, eating pizza for all three meals since the age of six. The main chef at Pizza Pizzaria is a retired marine biologist who spent three decades on a submarine. Though the depths of the ocean were fascinating he decided he missed the fresh tomatoes and melted cheese of pizza, and decided to spend the rest of his days making this delicious food. The rest of our staff are made up of various Olympic athletes who left their respective sports as they aged. Some of these Olymplians were adversaries in their sports, but they all come together to make our pizza.
